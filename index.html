Why We Built AutoBooks CA

Every day, millions of small shop owners across India spend hours manually entering bills, reconciling ledgers, and struggling to file GST — tasks that a Chartered Accountant would charge thousands of rupees a month to handle.

We at Kalbi Solutions Pvt. Ltd. built AutoBooks CA because we believe every shopkeeper deserves the same financial clarity that large businesses get. Whether you run a grocery store, electronics shop, medical store, or restaurant — your books should work for you, not against you.

AutoBooks CA takes every bill you generate — from any source — and automatically creates double-entry journal entries, calculates GST, updates your ledger, and gives you a real-time P&L statement. It's your CA, always on, always available, completely free.

Universal Connectors

Connect Every Billing App You Already Use

You don't need to switch your billing software. AutoBooks CA works with whatever you're already using — through four powerful methods designed for real-world Indian shopkeepers.

CSV / Excel Import — Export your sales register from Vyapar, Tally, Busy, Marg, or Zoho as a CSV file. Upload it to AutoBooks and every transaction is instantly booked with proper debit/credit entries and GST split. Column mapping is automatic and customisable.

Tally XML Import — If you use Tally Prime or ERP 9, simply export your Day Book as XML (Gateway → Display → Day Book → Export) and paste it into AutoBooks. All vouchers are parsed and posted in seconds.

WhatsApp / SMS Bill Parser — Received a bill on WhatsApp? Just paste the message text into our AI parser and it automatically extracts the vendor name, amount, bill number and books the entry — no manual typing required.

Live API Webhooks — For real-time automation, configure your billing app to POST to our webhook endpoint after every sale or purchase. AutoBooks books it instantly as it happens.

AI Intelligence

Your AI CA — Analysis at CA-Level Depth

Beyond bookkeeping, AutoBooks CA includes an AI-powered Chartered Accountant assistant that analyses your actual financial data and gives you sharp, actionable advice — just like a CA sitting across the table from you.

The AI looks at your real numbers — sales, purchases, expenses, receivables, payables — and generates insights on GST filing status, cash flow health, profit margin trends, pending payment risks and gives you one sharp recommendation to act on immediately.

⚡ How it works: The AI analysis uses the Anthropic Claude API. You bring your own free API key from console.anthropic.com — it's entered once in the app and stored only in your browser session. We never see or store your API key on our servers.

Privacy First

Your Financial Data Stays Completely Private

We designed AutoBooks CA with a privacy-first architecture. Your financial data — every invoice, every journal entry, every ledger balance — is processed entirely within your browser and never transmitted to our servers.

When you use the AI CA analysis feature, you enter your own Anthropic API key. This key is stored only in your current browser session memory. It is erased the moment you close or refresh the tab. Kalbi Solutions never receives, logs, or stores your API key or financial data.

Your accounting data belongs to you. Export it at any time. Delete it at any time. We have no access to it, period.

Smart Payments

Pay Vendors Directly From Your Books

AutoBooks CA tracks every pending vendor payment and lets you settle it without leaving the app. When a purchase bill is due, simply tap "Pay Now" — choose your preferred method and AutoBooks handles the rest.

UPI QR Code — Enter the vendor's UPI ID or mobile number and AutoBooks generates a QR code instantly. Open PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, or BHIM and scan it. On mobile, tap "Open UPI App" for a direct payment deep-link. The vendor's UPI details are saved for next time — one tap to pay again.

Bank Transfer / NEFT — Enter account number and IFSC once, get copy-ready payment details every time. Cheque — AutoBooks even writes the amount in words (e.g. "Twenty Five Thousand Rupees Only") for you. Every payment is automatically booked as a journal entry in your ledger.
